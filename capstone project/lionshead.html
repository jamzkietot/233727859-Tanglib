Lion's Head

The iconic symbol welcoming visitors to Baguio City.

About Lion's Head

The Lion’s Head is one of Baguio’s most famous landmarks located along Kennon Road. It stands about 40 feet tall and serves as a historic symbol welcoming travelers entering Baguio City. The site is a popular photo stop and souvenir shopping spot.

Visitor Information

  • 📍 Location: Kennon Road, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: Free
  • 🕕 Best Time to Visit: Morning or late afternoon

Nearby Places

  • 🏞️ Kennon Road Viewpoint
  • 🍽️ Cafés and snack stalls nearby
  • 🛍️ Souvenir shops
