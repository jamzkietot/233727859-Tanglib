Experience Adventure & Nature at Camp John Hay

Relax, dine, and explore the pine forest sanctuary of Baguio's most iconic destination.

About Camp John Hay

Camp John Hay is one of Baguio’s most beloved destinations, known for its pine forests, cafes, hotels, adventure parks, and historical charm. Once a rest & recreation facility for the U.S. military, it is now a scenic leisure destination perfect for family trips and nature lovers.

Visitor Information

  • 📍 Location: Loakan Road, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: Free (Some attractions may have fees)
  • 🕕 Best Time to Visit: Morning or late afternoon for cool weather and views

Popular Attractions

  • 🌲 Picnic areas & nature trails
  • ☕ The Manor & Forest Lodge hotels
  • ☁️ Eco Trail & Butterfly Sanctuary
  • 🛍️ Ayala Technohub & food spots
  • 🧗 Treetop Adventure Park
  • 🏌️ CJH Golf Course
