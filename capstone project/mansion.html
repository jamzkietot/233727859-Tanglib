About The Mansion

The Mansion is one of the most iconic landmarks in Baguio City. Built in 1908, it serves as the official summer residence of the President of the Philippines. With elegant Spanish-inspired architecture, wide gardens, and its famous grand gates, it remains one of the most photographed landmarks in the city.
