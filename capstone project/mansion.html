Explore The Mansion

Discover the historic summer residence of the Philippine President.

About The Mansion

The Mansion is one of the most iconic landmarks in Baguio City. Built in 1908, it currently serves as the official summer residence of the President of the Philippines. With elegant Spanish-inspired architecture, lush gardens, and grand iron gates, it is a must-visit historical attraction.

Visitor Information

  • 📍 Location: Leonard Wood Road, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: Free (Photo area only)
  • 🕕 Best Time: Morning for clearer views & cooler weather

Nearby Places

  • 🌲 Wright Park (Across the street)
  • ☕ Amare La Cucina (2 mins drive)
  • 🏞 Camp John Hay (5 mins drive)
