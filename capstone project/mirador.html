Mirador Heritage & Eco-Spirituality Park

Discover peace, history, and Instagram-worthy views of Baguio.

About Mirador Heritage Park

Mirador Heritage & Eco-Spirituality Park sits behind the Lourdes Grotto in Baguio City. It features the famous Torii Gate, stunning sunset spots, stone gardens, meditation spaces, and quiet nature trails.

Visitor Information

  • 📍 Location: Mirador Hill, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: ₱100 (as of 2025)
  • 🕕 Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM
