Discover the Serenity of Botanical Garden

Experience nature, culture, and peace in the heart of Baguio City.

About Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden in Baguio City is a lush sanctuary of native and imported plants. Visitors can enjoy peaceful walks, admire traditional Cordilleran sculptures, and explore native huts that reflect the rich heritage of the Cordillera region.

Visitor Information

  • 📍 Location: Leonard Wood Road, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: ₱10 (as of 2025)
  • 🕕 Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Nearby Restaurants & Attractions

  • ☕ Café Yagam – Local coffee & Cordilleran food (2 mins walk)
  • 🍲 Arca’s Yard – Scenic café & museum (5 mins drive)
  • 🍴 Secret Garden – Korean-Italian restaurant near Leonard Wood
  • 🏞️ Teacher’s Camp – Historic site nearby
