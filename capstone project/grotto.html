Visit the Lourdes Grotto

A sacred pilgrimage site offering peace and city views.

About Lourdes Grotto

The Our Lady of Lourdes Grotto is a famous religious shrine in Baguio City. Visitors climb 252 steps to reach the statue of the Blessed Virgin Mary, offering prayers and lighting candles. The site provides a peaceful atmosphere and a stunning view of the city once you reach the top.

Visitor Information

  • 📍 Location: Dominican Hill Road, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: Free
  • 🕕 Best Time: Early morning or sunset
