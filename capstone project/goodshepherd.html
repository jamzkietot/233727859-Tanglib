Good Shepherd Convent

Home of Baguio’s famous ube jam and a place of peace & charity.

About Good Shepherd

The Good Shepherd Convent in Baguio is famous for its high-quality products such as ube jam, strawberry jam, and other local delicacies. Every purchase supports the education of Cordillera youth — making every bite meaningful.

Visitor Information

  • 📍 Location: Outlook Drive, Baguio City
  • 🕕 Operating Hours: 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • 💰 Entry Fee: Free
  • 🎁 Famous For: Ube Jam, Strawberry Jam, Peanut Brittle

Nearby Attractions

  • 🏞️ Mines View Park (2 mins away)
  • ☕ Café by the Ruins Dua (10 mins drive)
  • 🍰 Arca’s Yard (5 mins drive)
