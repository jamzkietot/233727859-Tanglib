Witness the Scenic Beauty of Mines View Park

Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of Benguet's mountains and valleys.

About Mines View Park

Mines View Park is one of Baguio's most iconic viewing spots, offering a sweeping view of the old mining town of Itogon and the majestic Cordillera mountains. Enjoy photo ops, horseback riding, souvenir stalls, and local delicacies.

Visitor Information

  • 📍 Location: Mines View Road, Baguio City
  • 💰 Entrance Fee: ₱10 (as of 2025)
  • 🕕 Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Nearby Restaurants & Attractions

  • 🥤 Good Shepherd Convent – Famous Ube Jam (2 mins)
  • 🍽️ Local Souvenir Stalls – Silverworks, weaves, food pasalubong
  • 🐴 Horseback Riding Area near the entrance
